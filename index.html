About PodCoach

The Problem

Many schools are preparing their students for part-time or full-time remote learning. The remote sessions create problems for kids, who learn less effectively. They also create problems for working parents, including single parents and essential workers.

Many schools are getting pressure to host a significant population of students with full-time, in-person classes. This will be important for student learning, and for working parents. However, it conflicts with demands for reduced physical density, quarantine periods, and protection of teachers.

The Solution

PodCoach services provide trained coaches to your pod of 8-10 children. Operating under the principles of Essential Schooling, your student is the worker, our coach the mentor. We enhance the remote education experience by providing the tools to create an atmosphere where your student able to learn best.

Additionally, PodCoach provides training and tools for parents who wish to serve as mentors of a pod themselves. See our services below for more information.
